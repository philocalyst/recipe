Beet root made of Granny Smith apple covered with beet root powder

Servings
Author
More Data
Added: 2023-01-01

Ingredients

  • granny smith apple juice: 125 ml
  • protein_powder: 5.5 g
  • xanthan_gum: 5 mg
  • beet root powder

Cookware

  • blender
  • fridge
  • planet mixer
  • balloon whisk
  • piping bag
  • smooth nozzle
  • baking sheet
  • oven

Steps

  1. Mix granny smith apple juice with protein_powder and xanthan_gum with a blender.
  2. Cover it and let it sit for 2 h in the fridge.
  3. Beat the mass with a planet mixer (e.g. KitchenAid) with a balloon whisk to a strong meringue.
  4. Transfer the meringue into a piping bag with a smooth nozzle.
  5. Make small meringues onto the baking sheet in the shape of a beet root.
  6. Sprinkle with beet root powder (buy at a biological grocery store) and let dry in the oven at 70 C for 2 h.
